A little about me !

I call myself a neat coder as I always think a program without proper indentation and documentation is something which isn't useful in the future. It's like when I do coding I don't think about anything else and get deeply involved in it. Though I got attracted to professional web developing not much time ago (3 years ), but I feel so deeply attached to it that I always think about it. It gives me great pleasure to work on projects. Three years of experience gave me a good knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Angular 2. On the database side, I learned MySql. The first professional database I made was a university conference management database which made me quite confident in building a good MySQL database. Learned GitHub to better fit in a company's team.
